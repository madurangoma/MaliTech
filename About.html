About maliTECH

maliTECH is a technology solutions company based in Lusaka, Zambia, focused on building modern digital experiences tailored for the Zambian and African market. We specialize in website development, mobile app interfaces, e-commerce platforms, and branding solutions for businesses of all sizes.

Our mission is to empower local businesses and startups through affordable, professional, and scalable tech solutions. With a team of skilled developers, designers, and marketers, maliTECH strives to bridge the digital gap across sectors through innovation.
